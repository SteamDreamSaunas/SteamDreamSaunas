Material & Sustainability

Lithuanian alder wood (Standard)

High-quality, slow-grown spruce. Light in color with a natural look. Requires occasional treatment (stain/oil) for longevity.

Thermowood (Recommended Upgrade)

Wood that has been thermally treated (heated without chemicals).

  • Lasts for years without rot or mold
  • Better insulation value (retains heat)
  • Hardly warps or shrinks
  • Wonderful smoky scent
